Simon Lowe

A brand-new site for a broadcaster going freelance — two design directions exploring how to put his personality on screen.

Design Copywriting Development

// The brief

Simon is a radio presenter and event host going freelance — which means clients now come looking directly, and with no site there was nowhere for them to land. A presenter sells personality as much as skill, so I explored two directions rather than one, giving Simon a real choice about how he comes across before a build.

// What I did

  • Two full design directions
  • Homepage copy and tone of voice
  • Showreel & audio-led layouts
  • Booking enquiry flow
  • Responsive design across both routes
  • Build-ready specification

Two directions explored

Option one

A light, warm treatment — off-white studio tones with the station photography carrying the energy. Friendly and approachable, closer to how Simon sounds on air day to day.

Option two

The same structure in a dark, broadcast-booth palette — moodier and more premium, leaning into the on-air feel for the corporate and event side of the work.
